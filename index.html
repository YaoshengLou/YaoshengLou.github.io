About Me

A quick introduction

Image generated by ChatGPT (DALL·E)

I am currently a PhD student in Eduional Psychology and Methodology at the University at Albany, SUNY. My research focuses on methodological innovations using advanced statistical methods and AI-based approaches to enhance research workflows and data analysis in evidence synthesis.

With a background in computer science, I’m deeply interested in how artificial intelligence can enhance research quality and efficiency. I am currently developing an AI chatbot for meta-analysis of single-case experimental designs (SCEDs), from research question formulation to screening, data extraction, risk of bias, and effect size selection. Feel free to reach out if you have ideas or are interested in collaborating!

In my spare time, I enjoy finding creative ways to explain complex statistics without putting students to sleep, having serious conversations with ChatGPT, and daydreaming about a world where code always runs on the first try...
