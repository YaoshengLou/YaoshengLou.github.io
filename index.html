About Me

A quick introduction

I am currently a PhD candidate in Educational Psychology and Methodology at the University at Albany, State University of New York. My research focuses on methodological innovations using advanced statistical methods and AI-based approaches to enhance research workflows and data analysis in evidence synthesis.

With a background in computer science, I am deeply interested in how artificial intelligence can enhance research quality and efficiency. I am currently developing an AI chatbot for meta-analysis of single-case experimental designs (SCEDs), from research question formulation to screening, data extraction, risk of bias, and effect size selection. I am also committed to improving the evaluation of interventions and therapies through the integration of advanced quantitative methods.
